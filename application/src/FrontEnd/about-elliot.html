Elliot Warren - Team Lead

Elliot Warren is a recent graduate of San Francisco State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. He is passionate about building personal projects that will help automate tedious tasks in life. Elliot has four years of military service that tempered his skills in discipline, leadership, and adaptability, which he is now eager to apply to the tech industry. Elliot has led two teams of four, and a team of 6 in learning-based coding projects throughout his time at San Francisco State University. Outside of his professional endeavors, Elliot enjoys building aquariums, playing video games, and experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. He is driven by a desire to make a positive impact through technology and is constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow in his field.
